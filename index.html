WHY PRACTICE, WHEN YOU CAN PLAY?
Anyone who's ever attempted mastering an instrument knows that it takes hours of practice before things get remotely fun. This is the hard part - and usually enough to extinguish even the most excited player's ambition.
Help is on the way: JazzJammr is an app for iPad and iPhone that allows players of any instrument to practice playing along with professional bands.
The app is free and comes with a few basic tracks. Through our library musicians can purchase additional tracks. Simply select a track, plug in their headphones, and start jamming.
